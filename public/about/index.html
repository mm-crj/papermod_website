About

About section of my website.

I am a PhD student at the Institute of Scientific Computing, TU Dresden. I work with Markus Schmidtschen.

Research#

My research primarily involve questions in biology, particularly in the field of systems involving interacting species. The particles can interact through various influences of Non-linear Diffusion, drift due to external potential and non local interactions amongst themselves. I look at these systems at a macro level, in the mean field limit. At this level the system is described by a system of coupled Partial Differential Equations. The PDEs are usually conservation laws. I try to find weak solutions to these laws using Finite Volume Schemes. I try to find schemes that are convergent, consistent and preserve the properties of the scheme from continuous level.

About#

I obtained my Integrated Bachelors and Masters of Science in Mathematics in 2017 from the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Kolkata with a INSPIRE- SHE Scholarship. My masters thesis was supervised by Associate Professor Dr. Kuntal Ghosh, Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. At that point in my career I wanted to do something more applied, hence I joined a Erusmus Mundus Engineering Mathematics Masters program called Mathmods, jointly organised by University of L’aquila, Italy and University of Hamburg, Germany. My masters thesis was supervised by Professor Dr. Nicola Guglielmi from the Gran sasso Research Institute, L’aquila. I graduated my second masters in 2020. Due to the pandemic and other personal reasons I took a break from my career for about a year or so. Then, I joined Institute of Scientific Computing, TU Dresden in September 2022 to work with Junior Professor Dr. Markus Schmidtchen.
